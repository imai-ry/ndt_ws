

a. LiDARとカメラを起動しNDT-SLAMを行う. カメラの色情報は使っておらず, 点群の情報だけでSLAMを行う.

1. check workspace 
$ vi ~/.bashrc

2. check transform (base to lidar) and modify ndt_mapping.launch

3. 
(usb_cam)
$ roslaunch ndt_mapping ndt_jikken.launch
$ cd /path/to/savefile/
$ rosbag record -a -x "(.*)compressed(.*)"

(uvc_cam)
$ roslaunch ndt_mapping ndt_jikken.launch
$ ctrl + c
$ sudo chmod 666 /dev/bus/usb/???/???
$ roslaunch ndt_mapping ndt_jikken.launch
